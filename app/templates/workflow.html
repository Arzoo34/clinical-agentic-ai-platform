AI Workflow

  1. Ingest: PySpark reads all study folders, standardizes columns, and maps subjects to sites.
  2. Engineer: Compute open issues, SAE counts, missing pages/labs, coding backlog, inactivated forms, and visit delays per site.
  3. Label: Rule-based risk scores flag high-risk sites to bootstrap interpretable ML.
  4. Train: Logistic regression learns drivers of risk; metrics exported for review.
  5. Explain: Generative layer converts metrics + probabilities into English narratives.
  6. Act: Agentic rules create alerts and Power BI-ready CSV/Parquet feeds.
